- Log in to Discord at Discord.com.
- After successfully logging in, press F12 to open the Developer Tools.
- Go to the Network tab and type /api in the search bar.
- A list of files will appear. Find @me and click on it.
- In the Headers section, scroll down and look for Authorization:.
